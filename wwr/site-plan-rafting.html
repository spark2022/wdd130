Overview

Purpose

Rafting Travel is a company created for those who enjoy adventure sports and white water sailing while working as a team.

Audience

Although it is an risk sport, it is a controlled risk sport directed by experts and adapted to the needs of children, youth and adults who want to work as a team. These can be made up of families, school or university groups, or people who work for institutions, companies, etc.
