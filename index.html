Venezuela

Venezuela is the country further to the North of South America and is known for having diverse climates, from snowing mountains to tropical forests, sunny beaches, plains, and even deserts, all at the same time. Nevertheless, during the year, Venezuela doesn't have four seasons like other countries, just a rainy season and a dry season. And so as it has diverse climates, it has a very high biodiversity and ranks seventh in the world list of nations with the largest number of species. Its crowdest city is Caracas the capital, but although it can be hit by tropical storms in the Atlantic Ocean, it is protected by mountains, the best known, The Avila or Waraira Repano since where, at the top of the mountain, it can see the city in the valley at one side, and the sea at the other side of the mountain. It is something worth seeing.
